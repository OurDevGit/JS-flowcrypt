Fixing Google Chrome Content Settings

Please review Chrome Settings -> Additional Settings -> Privacy -> Content Settings
Or open the settings page directly at chrome://settings/content

Simple fix

Un-check Block third-party cookies and site data in Cookies section
When the checkbox is empty, close your web mail window and open it again.
